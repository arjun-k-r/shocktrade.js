Business Summary
Alternative Energy Partners, Inc. is focused on sourcing, marketing and distributing renewable alternative energy solutions. AEGY's family of businesses includes: Xnergy (www.xnergy.com), an Energy Services Company, construction and engineering firm; Sunaria Corporation (www.sunarias.com), provider of on-site solar-thermal energy systems; Shovon, LLC (www.shovon.com), a developer of remote control energy management systems; and Skynet Energy Systems, Inc., through which it is poised to enter the European alternative energy market.
